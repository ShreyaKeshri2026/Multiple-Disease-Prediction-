🌍 Real-World Disease Statistics in India

💓 Heart Disease

  • Accounts for ~27% of all deaths in India. [Source]
  • Highest prevalence in the 40-69 age group.
  • Often undetected until late stages.

🍬 Diabetes

  • 101 million+ Indians living with diabetes. [Source]
  • ~57% of cases remain undiagnosed.
  • Expected to hit 134 million by 2045.

🎗️ Breast Cancer

  • Most common cancer in Indian women. [Source]
  • Early detection boosts survival to 90%+
  • More than 200,000 new cases annually.
